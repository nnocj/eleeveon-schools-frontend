# Eleeveon Platform Team Portal

This folder is a staff operations portal for people enrolled to help with Eleeveon. It is not the Developer Portal.

It includes safe workflows only: support, accounts read view, sync help, billing support, bugs, QA, releases, knowledge base, team members, permissions, activity logs, announcements, work notes and team settings.

It intentionally excludes raw SQL, database reset, migration runner, secret API key exposure and dangerous owner-only controls.
