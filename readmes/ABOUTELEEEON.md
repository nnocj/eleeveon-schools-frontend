 Eleeveon Systems is a technology company focused on building connected digital systems for learning, work, and life. The company develops modern cloud-enabled and offline-capable platforms that help schools, organizations, and businesses manage operations efficiently. Eleeveon’s solutions include school management systems, academic analytics, finance and billing tools, reporting engines, and integrated communication platforms. With a strong focus on accessibility, scalability, and innovation, Eleeveon aims to empower institutions across Africa through reliable technology solutions designed for real-world environments, including mobile-first experiences, offline synchronization, and secure digital payments using platforms such as Paystack and Mobile Money services.
